Power Electronics Research Intern
05/2024 to present @U of T Lab for Advanced Power Conversion and Systems Analysis

700 VDC Half-Bridge PCB

I'm using LTspice, Altium Designer, and PLECS to design a high-power half-bridge PCB employing cutting-edge GaN HEMT technology. For this prototype, we're focusing on the bootstrapped gate driver, which we're level-shifting and controlling with HRPWM signals from a DSP. Since GaN gates are sensitive to noise, there is a great need to minimize trace inductance, EMI, and Miller plateau.
