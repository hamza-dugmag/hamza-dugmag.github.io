(2021) U of T Aerospace Team · Rocketry Division

Avionics Ground System

I designed and built the avionics system used to initiate and control launch procedures for our hybrid and liquid engine rockets. It features power electronics, a Raspberry Pi loaded with a custom user interface, a data acquisition board for load cells and pressure transducers, a servo motor board, a relay board for solenoid valves, and a wireless transceiver.

(2021) Praxis II Showcase

VoiceEvo

For my first-year capstone project, I collaborated with four peers to design a voice-activated release system for ski airbags. I prototyped Bowden cable pulling mechanisms, created testing protocols, and presented our design to students, stakeholders, other engineers, and U of T faculty members.
