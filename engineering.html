(2021) U of T Aerospace Team · Rocketry

Liquid Rocket Flight System

In preparation for the Launch Canada Competition, I designed a custom finite-state machine for controlling our liquid rocket in flight. It features abort and redundancy systems as well as a centralized data package for communication with avionics input and output devices.

(2021) Praxis II Showcase

VoiceEvo

For the cornerstone Year 1 EngSci project, I collaborated with four peers to design a voice-activated ski airbag triggering system. We prototyped Bowden-cable pulling mechanisms, created testing protocols, and presented our design to students, stakeholders, other engineers, and U of T faculty members.

(2020) CIV102 Project

Matboard Box-Girder Bridge

I collaborated with three peers to develop two bridge designs that span a valley while supporting a moving load. Given load and material requirements, we designed statically determinate and indeterminate box-girder bridges.
