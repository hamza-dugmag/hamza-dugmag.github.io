(2021) U of T Aerospace Team · Rocketry

Liquid Rocket Flight System

In preparation for the Launch Canada Competition, I designed a custom finite-state machine for controlling our liquid rocket in flight. It features abort and redundancy systems as well as a centralized data package for communication with avionics input and output devices.

For the cornerstone Year 1 EngSci project, I collaborated with four peers to design a voice-activated ski airbag triggering system.
